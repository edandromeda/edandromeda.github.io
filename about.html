ABOUT

As mentioned on the main page, video games should be fun. Although I say this, the online world is still dangerous and should be taken with precautions. Practicing online safety is essential to your safety and enjoyment.

Cause:

→ Everyone should feel welcomed while playing online video games.

→ For those who have become victims to harassment and dangerous events, there are plenty of resources to assist.

→ The video game industry, while not being all, has a majority of misogynistic, homophobic, and transphobic harassers. Those being harassed can learn how to react to such acts and to follow the resources and hotlines provided after the fact.

Why The Cause Is Relevent:

→ The Internet can be a dangerous place.

→ People deserve to have support if they are experiencing anxiety inducing scenarios.

→ Those who are harassing could be held accountable and possibly change.

Reflection:

1. Women and LGBTQ+ are statistically more harassed while playing video games.

2. Some harassers reach out to the hotline to let out anger from their game.

3. Just being there for a friend can help when they are struggling.

Audience to Reach:

→ Women

→ LGBTQ+

→ People of Color

→ New Online Players

→ Children and Adolescence

Solutions:

→ Remove personal information off the internet.

→ Provide support for those in need.

→ Provide more awareness to the cause.

→ Decrease the amount of harassers online.

Conclusion:

→ Practice safe habits on the internet and online games.


Stay Safe :)
